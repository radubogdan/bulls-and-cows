      About

      Bulls and cows is an old code-breaking mind or paper and pencil game for two or more players. It's a lot like the commercial game Mastermind but you'll play with digits instead of pegs. For this implementation, you have to guess the computer 4 digit number. The number has always unique digits and can't start with 0.

      Buttons:
      Start: Start the game
      Clear: Clear the guess field
      Reset: Clear the guess field and clean the table
